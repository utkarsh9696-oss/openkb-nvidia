## Answer

**Sending Notifications to Parents: A Step-by-Step Guide**

To send a notification to parents, you can use the school app to create and publish notifications that are clear, concise, and easily accessible to the wider school community. According to the document "how-to-send-a-notification-2026-02-18.pdf", sending notifications is a crucial aspect of effective communication in a school setting.

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. Go to Manage Notifications and click Add to create a new notification.
2. Enter a title and message.
3. Send as a push notification (device alert) to selected users.
4. Add to a category (optional).
5. Add images (optional).
6. Add video (optional).
7. Add a PDF (optional).
8. Schedule and archive (optional).
9. Redirect to a specified area of the app.
