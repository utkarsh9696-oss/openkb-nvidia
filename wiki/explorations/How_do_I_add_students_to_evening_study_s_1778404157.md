# Query: How do I add students to evening study sessions?

**Date:** 2026-05-10 14:39:17

## Answer

**Adding Students to Evening Study Sessions: A Step-by-Step Guide**

To add students to evening study sessions, you can follow these steps:

1. Create a new evening study session or access an existing one using the Unique School App.
2. Select the students you want to add to the session from the year/class or groups.
3. You can also create groups based on payment status, such as fully paid or instalment payments, to easily add students to the session.
