## Overview
Notification categories are a feature used to organize and streamline the Notifications feed, allowing users to display only relevant content in each category. This feature is particularly useful for schools, where notifications can be sub-categorized by year, enabling parents and guardians to easily access information relevant to their student. By creating notification categories, users can efficiently manage and prioritize their notifications.

## Key Concepts
* Notification categories
* Sub-categorization
* Sort order
* Notification feed
* Dashboard
* Notifications

## Main Topics Covered
### Adding a Notification Category
To add a notification category, users must navigate to the Notifications section on their Dashboard, click on Notification Categories, and then click the Add button. They must then enter a title for the category and choose a sort order, which determines the category's display position across the top of the feed.

### Notification Categories and Sort Order
The sort order applies to the display position of each category, with lower numbers appearing first. For example, if "1st Year" is assigned a sort order of 7 and "2nd Year" is assigned a sort order of 6, "2nd Year" will appear before "1st Year" in the feed.

### Applying Categories to Notifications
Once a category is created, it can be applied to notifications, allowing users to organize and filter their notifications efficiently.

## Important Relationships and Connections
The notification categories feature is closely related to the Notifications feed, as it allows users to organize and prioritize their notifications. The sort order is also an essential aspect of notification categories, as it determines the display position of each category. Additionally, the Dashboard and Notifications section are interconnected, as users must navigate to the Notifications section on their Dashboard to access and manage notification categories.
