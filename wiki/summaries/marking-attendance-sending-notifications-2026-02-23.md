## Marking Attendance and Sending Notifications
Marking attendance and sending notifications is a crucial process for staff members in charge of Evening Study sessions. This process involves taking roll call and informing parents/guardians about their child's attendance through Personal Notifications. The Unique School App provides a user-friendly interface for staff members to mark attendance and send notifications efficiently.

## Key Concepts
* Marking attendance for Evening Study sessions
* Sending Personal Notifications to parents/guardians
* Using the Unique School App for attendance management
* Filtering attendance by present or absent students
* Using templates for sending notifications

## Main Topics Covered
### Marking Attendance
Marking attendance involves logging into the Unique School App, navigating to the Evening Study section, and locating the relevant session. Staff members can then mark each student as present or absent.

### Sending Notifications
After marking attendance, staff members can send notifications to parents/guardians using the Unique School App. This involves filtering attendance by present or absent students and using templates for sending notifications.

### Using the Unique School App
The Unique School App provides a user-friendly interface for staff members to manage attendance and send notifications. Staff members can log in, navigate to the Evening Study section, and access various features such as marking attendance and sending notifications.
