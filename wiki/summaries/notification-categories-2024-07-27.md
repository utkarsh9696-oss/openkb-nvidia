## Overview
Notification categories are a feature used to organize and filter notifications in a feed, making it easier for users to find relevant information. By creating sub-categories such as Meetings, Trips, or Procedures, users can display only the content that is pertinent to their needs. This feature is particularly useful in educational settings, where schools can sub-categorize notifications by year to help parents and guardians quickly identify information relevant to their student.

## Key Concepts
* Notification categories: sub-categories used to organize notifications in a feed
* Sort Order: determines the display order of categories across the top of the feed
* Dashboard: the main interface where users can access and manage notifications
* Notifications feed: the area where all notifications are displayed
* Sub-categorization: the process of dividing a category into smaller, more specific groups (e.g., by year)

## Main Topics Covered
### Adding a Notification Category
This topic covers the step-by-step process of creating a new notification category, including accessing the Notifications menu, clicking on Notification Categories, and submitting a new category title and sort order.

### Notification Categories and Sort Order
This topic explains how the Sort Order feature works and how it affects the display of categories in the notifications feed. It highlights the importance of choosing the correct sort order to ensure that categories are displayed in a logical and user-friendly manner.

### Applying Categories to Notifications
This topic discusses how to apply newly created categories to notifications, making it easier for users to filter and find relevant information in the notifications feed.

## Important Relationships and Connections
The concept of notification categories is closely related to the idea of filtering and organizing information. By creating sub-categories, users can reduce information overload and quickly find the information they need. The Sort Order feature is also crucial, as it determines the display order of categories and affects the overall user experience. Additionally, the ability to apply categories to notifications is essential, as it enables users to take full advantage of the categorization feature.
