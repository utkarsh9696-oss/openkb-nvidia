## Overview
The Access & Setup guide provides a step-by-step approach to accessing and managing the Evening Study feature on the Unique Schools system. Written by Sam Support and last published on February 23, 2026, this guide enables schools to set up a flexible system that suits their needs. It covers the process of accessing Evening Study, managing Time Slots, and creating sessions, ensuring that schools can effectively utilize the feature.

## Key Concepts
* Accessing the Evening Study feature via the Year Head Dashboard
* Managing Evening Study Time Slots
* Creating and deleting Time Slots
* Customizing Time Slot names and time ranges
* Utilizing the Unique Schools system for flexible setup

## Main Topics Covered
### Accessing Evening Study
Accessing Evening Study is done by logging into the Year Head Dashboard on the Unique Schools system and clicking on the Evening Study option near the bottom right of the screen. If the option is not visible, users can contact staff support to have it enabled.

### Managing Time Slots
Managing Time Slots involves clicking on the Settings icon in the Evening Study section and entering the desired Time Slot name and time range. Users can add new Time Slots by clicking the "Add Time Slot" button and delete existing ones by clicking the Bin icon beside the Time Slot.

### Creating Sessions
Although not explicitly covered in the guide, creating sessions is implied to be a part of managing Evening Study Time Slots. By setting up Time Slots, users can create sessions that suit their school's needs.
