## Overview
Sending notifications is a crucial aspect of effective communication in a school setting. The document "how-to-send-a-notification-2026-02-18.pdf" provides a step-by-step guide on how to send school-wide notifications using the school app. This guide is designed to help users create and publish notifications that are clear, concise, and easily accessible to the wider school community.

## Key Concepts
* Notifications are public posts within the app, visible to all registered users
* Notifications are used for important, school-wide announcements such as school closures, weather warnings, and exam information
* Keeping News and Notifications as separate feeds helps parents distinguish between important and "nice to know" updates
* Notifications can be customized with images, videos, and PDFs to increase engagement
* Notifications can be scheduled, archived, and redirected to specific areas of the app

## Main Topics Covered
### Creating and Sending a Notification
The guide provides a 12-step process for creating and sending a notification, including:
1. Going to Manage Notifications and clicking Add to create a new notification
2. Entering a title and message
3. Sending as a push notification (device alert) to selected users

5. Adding images (optional)
6. Adding video (optional)
7. Adding a PDF (optional)
8. Scheduling and archiving (optional)
9. Redirecting to a specified area of the app (optional)
10. Posting to social media (optional)
11. Sending as an SMS (optional)
12. Clicking Send to publish the notification

### Customization Options
The guide highlights various customization options, including:
* Adding images, videos, and PDFs to increase engagement
* Scheduling and archiving notifications to control when they appear and disappear
* Redirecting to specific areas of the app to improve navigation and user experience
* Posting to social media to reach a wider audience

### Important Considerations
The guide emphasizes the importance of:
* Keeping notifications concise and clear
* Using notifications for important, school-wide announcements only
* Avoiding individual or confidential communication through notifications
* Using categories to organize notifications and make them easier to find

## Important Relationships and Connections
The guide highlights the connection between notifications and other features of the school app, such as:
* News and Notifications as separate feeds
* Categories and sub-categories for organizing notifications
* Social media integration for posting notifications to Facebook, Twitter, and the school website
* SMS integration for notifying non-app users
